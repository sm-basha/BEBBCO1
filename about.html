About Us

B E B B C O

BEBBCO Group is known as diversified businesses in this region for last 36 years. Started with the establishment of BHARAT ENGG. & BODY BUILDING CO. PVT. LTD. in the Year 1983, The group headed by our
Chairman Shree Gajanand Bhalotia .

BEBBCO specializes in fabrication of "Automobile Body". We build customized body for automobile depending on the purpose for the vehicle.

At BEBBCO we have fabricated the following Automobile bodies on TATA, EICHER, Ashok Leyland, BHARAT BENZ, URAL Chassis ranging from 2600 WB to 6200 WB for domestic as well as for export market.


“We make anything on Wheels”

Is dedicated towards manufacturing of Passenger as well as Goods Vehicle for providing safest and comfortable journey to its passenger while constantly strive for improvement in productivity and bring down cost of operations.

Will try to join the league of the best in the industry while catering to the need of customers, employees and society and set new benchmarks with our quality, dedication and teamwork.
